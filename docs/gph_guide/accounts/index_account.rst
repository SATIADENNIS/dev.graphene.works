********************
Graphene Accounts
********************

In Graphene Blockchain, you can create an account relatively easily by using Graphene UI wallet.  As a default, you will create a Cloud Wallet. You can imagine the Cloud Wallet smiler to your normal bank account. You remember your account name and a password and the bank manage and save your funds for you.

After you create, a Graphene wallet and an account.  We recommend you to look through the Graphene wallet options and functionalities.
In this section, we introduce you some of the important functionalities to get familiar with.
